Developer Student Club Kabarak University is a community based group for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining us, you'll grow your knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
